Emoji Communication

Communication Transformation

There’s no doubt that the introduction of emoji as a communication tool has transformed the way we interact. I’m sure I’m not alone in using at least one a day to convey some thought, idea, or reaction...mostly reaction (let’s be honest). In our increasingly digital, fast-paced lives adapting to more stream-lined communication methods is critical. But is emoji communication the best way to convey your message?

Miscommunications happen all the time with text language and emoji is no different. At times, the differences between emoji are so nuanced that they become hard to distinguish. For example, what one person perceives as the ‘grin’ face another person might think is the ‘grimace’ face. There can also be miscommunications stemming from generational, cultural, and ethnic differences that can be inflated by emoji usage. For better or worse, emoji are here to stay and we must adapt to keep up.
